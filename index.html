How to read this table
Short = put you sell (collect premium)  ·  Long = put you buy (caps your loss)  ·  Credit = net premium kept if expires worthless  ·  Max Loss = (width − credit) × 100 per contract  ·  B/E = QQQ must stay above this at expiration  ·  RoR% = credit ÷ max loss — higher is better  ·  OTM = pts below QQQ (more = safer, less credit)
